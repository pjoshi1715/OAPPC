Our Product



The Problem

In the USA, over 130 people die each day due to an opioid overdose. This problem affects every level of society today and costs the government 78.5 Billion dollars a year. People get addicted to opioids by receiving a prescription dose of an opioid painkiller like (OxyContin, Vicodin, or Morphine) and take too much of their dose when they encounter more pain/trouble. We wanted to solve this problem by proposing an alternative solution that makes the user strictly adhere to their prescription and hold them accountable by having it controlled by the doctors who give them their prescriptions.

Our Solution

Our solution is a secure pill dispenser, that acts like a gumball machine, and only dispenses 1 or 2 pills at a specified time that the doctors set for the user. This solution bridges the gap between the prescription and the user.

Why Pick Us

  • Cheaper than alternatives
  • Much more efficient than alternatives at preventing overdoses
  • Can be used for other pills than opioids
  • We have a companion app that pairs with the container that uses Face ID to authenticate users, and can even reminds you to take your medications if you forget
